Grilled Chicken

Description

Juicy, flavorful chicken marinated with spices and grilled until perfectly charred.
Great as a main course or protein for salads and wraps.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs or breasts
  • Olive oil
  • 1 tabele spoon of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice,garlic,herbs,salt and pepper
  2. Coat the chicken in the marinade and let sit for at least 30 minutes
  3. Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium-high heat
  4. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes per side
  5. Let rest for a few minutes before serving
  6. Serve with rice,salad or vegetables

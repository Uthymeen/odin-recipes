Fried Rice

Description

A delicious stir-fried dish made with pre-cooked rice, vegetables, eggs, and optionally chicken, beef,
or shrimp. Itâ€™s a quick and customizable meal.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Oil
  • Vegetables
  • Salt and pepper
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until transluscent
  3. Add mixed veggetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes
  4. Push veggies to one side and scramble the beaten eggs on the other side
  5. Add cooked rice to the pan and mix everything together
  6. Add soy sauce,salt and pepper. Stir well
  7. Add cooked meat and mix until heated through
  8. Serve hot

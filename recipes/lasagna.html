Lasagna

Description

A hearty Italian layered pasta dish with meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel (or ricotta),
and lots of melted cheese. Comfort food at its finest.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • ground beef or sausage
  • 2 cups tomato sauce or marinara
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until dente, then drain
  2. In a pan, cook onions and garlic until soft
  3. Add ground meat, cook until browned. Add tomato sauce and simmer
  4. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius
  5. In a baking dish,spread a thin layer of sauce
  6. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta,then sauce,then mozzarella
  7. Repaeat layers
  8. Finish with a layer of sauce and top with Parmesan cheese
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until bubbly and golden
  10. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing
